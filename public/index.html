About Me

My entrance to the beauty industry started in 2006, with a main focus and concentration on hair. I specialize in haircutting and coloring for men and women, as well as event hairstyling, fusion bond hair extensions, and keratin infusion smoothing treatments. I consistently strive to create a memorable experience for my clients, starting with an in-depth consultation to achieve the best style combination to fit your lifestyle and maintenance routines. I ask many questions, listen to your individual needs, and consider your preferences and past experiences to satisfy your hair goals. I love to share my knowledge of styling tips and tricks so my clients have the confidence to recreate the look they leave the salon with.. plus more!

Education

Since graduating from the European Institute of Cosmetology in Portland Oregon, my passion for hair has expanded within ongoing education classes through the years. I have gained inspiration through training with Bumble and Bumble, Goldwell, Wella, So.Cap USA hair extensions, Keratin Complex, Oribe, Davines, Joico, Unite, and Kevin Murphy to name a few. I find that the more knowledge I collect through education, the more I can offer my clients to be on-point, precise, and up to date with current trends. I look forward to sharing my experience and expertise with you!
